Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Download AllTeacher, sign in with Apple, and tap "Create Curriculum". Choose your target language, your current level, and your learning goal — AllTeacher will build a personalised plan in seconds.

What languages does AllTeacher support?

AllTeacher supports all major world languages including Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Mandarin, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Russian, and many more.

What is adaptive re-planning?

AllTeacher monitors your performance after each exercise. If you're consistently struggling with a topic, it automatically adjusts your upcoming lessons to spend more time on it. If you're breezing through content, it accelerates your plan.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, find AllTeacher, and tap "Cancel Subscription". You can also tap "Manage Subscription" inside the app. Your subscription stays active until the end of the billing period.

I switched to a new iPhone — how do I restore my purchases?

Open AllTeacher, go to the Subscription screen, and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.

Can I use AllTeacher on multiple devices?

Yes. Sign in with the same Apple ID on any iPhone and your curricula, progress, and subscription will all be synced automatically.

Is my data private?

Yes. We do not sell your data or use it to train AI models without your consent. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account directly inside the app. Scroll to the bottom of the Home screen and tap Delete account. You will be asked to confirm twice. Once confirmed, your account, curricula, progress, and all associated data are permanently deleted immediately — this cannot be undone. If you have an active subscription, cancel it first in Settings → Subscriptions to avoid future charges.

The app is crashing or not loading — what should I do?

Try force-quitting the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, check that your iOS is up to date. If you're still experiencing problems, email us with your device model and iOS version and we'll look into it.

I was charged but the app shows the Free plan

Go to the Subscription screen in the app and tap Restore Purchases. If that doesn't resolve it, contact us with your Apple ID email (not your password) and we'll investigate.
