Subscription Terms

Last updated: May 2026

AllTeacher offers optional auto-renewing subscriptions that unlock additional features. This page explains exactly what you get with each plan, how billing works, and how to cancel.

How Billing Works

  • Subscriptions are purchased and billed through the Apple App Store. Payment is charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase.
  • Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing period (monthly) unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the renewal date.
  • Your Apple ID account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period.
  • You can manage and turn off auto-renewal in your iPhone's App Store account settings at any time.
  • No refunds are provided for the current subscription period upon cancellation, except as required by applicable law.

Free Trial

AllTeacher does not currently offer a free trial on paid plans. All plans begin billing immediately upon purchase. The Free plan is available indefinitely with no credit card required.

How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time directly from your iPhone:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top → Subscriptions
  3. Find AllTeacher and tap it
  4. Tap Cancel Subscription

Alternatively, you can tap Manage Subscription inside the AllTeacher app on the Subscription screen.

After cancellation, your subscription remains active until the end of the paid period. After that, your account reverts to the Free plan and you retain all your learning data.

Upgrading or Downgrading

You can upgrade your plan at any time — the change takes effect immediately and Apple will prorate the billing accordingly. Downgrading takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

Restoring Purchases

If you reinstall AllTeacher or switch to a new iPhone, tap Restore Purchases on the Subscription screen to recover your active subscription. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used to purchase.
